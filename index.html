分词（形容词性动词）

分词即具有形容词性的动词变形

注意：

  1. 第四变位法动词的现在词干变形为 -ie- （eg：audio → audiens，audiendus）
  2. 现在时主动态分词变格法同第三变格法形容词（i-词干第三变格法名词），唯单数夺格结尾可能是 -e/-i：若分词单独出现则为-i；非单独出现则为-e。（eg：a patre amanti / a patre filium amante）
  3. 其余分词变形同第一第二变格法形容词。

名词性动词

不定式

  1. 做主语
  2. 做 debere(debeo)、posse(possum) 等的补充
  3. 做直接宾语 （eg：Iussit eos venire. He orders them to come.）
  4. 在 dico(say), puto(think) 后面作为间接引语。（eg：Iulia putat se esse bellam puellam. Julia thinks that she is a beautiful girl. ）

名词与形容词

  • 主格：主语
  • 属格：表示另一个名词属于这个名词，一般后置
  • 与格：间接宾语
  • 宾格：直接宾语
  • 夺格：状语中，一般在介词后
  • 呼格：单独成句直接称呼时

i ：i-词干第三变格法名词，包括f/m（主属）等音节词（civis, civis），f/m（词根结尾）双辅音词（urbs, urbis），主格以-e、-al、-ar结尾的中性词（mare, maris），不规则词（vis, vis）

a：三变形容词的其他形式同i-干三变名词，唯有单数夺格以 -i 结尾

5：第五变格法有特例 dies(day) 和 meridies(midday) 是阳性，其余为阴性。当-e-前面为元音时，-e-变长音。

代词

人称代词

表示属格 my, our, your 时总是用形容词（要变格的） meus, -a, -um: my tuus, -a, -um: your noster, -tra, -trum: our vester, -tra, -trum: your

idem, eadem, idem (the same thing)的特殊变形 单主 idem, eadem, idem 单宾 eundem, eandem, idem 复属 eorundem, earundem, eorundem

第三人称的反身代词(无主，单复同) 属 sui 与 sibi 宾 se 夺 se 属格形容词 suus, -a, -um

强势代词 ipse, -a, -um 用作同位语表强调 单属 ipsius, ipsius, ipsius 单与 ipsi, ipsi, ipsi 其他同形容词变换

关系代词

在定语从句中指代先行词 qui, quae=who, that quod=which, that 关系代词的性和数由其先行词决定，格则由它在从句中的用法来决定

疑问代词

quis=who, quid=what 后可加具体名词 e.g: quem librum = what book

状语

带介词夺格

  1. cum 表伴随

    Cum amico id scripsit. He wrote it with the friend.

  2. cum 表方式

    Cum cura id scripsit. He wrote it with care.

    Magna cum cura id scripsit. He wrote it with great care.

  3. in, sub 表在何处

    In urbe id scripsit. He wrote it in the city.

  4. ab(a), de, ex(e) 表从何处

    Ex urbe id misit. He sent it from a city.

  5. ab(a), de, ex(e) 表分离

    Ab urbe eos prohibuit. He kept them (away) from the city.

  6. ab 表施事

    Ab amico id scriptum est. It was written by a friend.

  7. ex(e), de 放基数词后表整体

    Tres ex navibus discesserunt. Three of the ships departed.

无介词夺格

  1. 手段

    Sua manu id scripsit. He wrote it with his own hand.

  2. 方式（若方式名词有形容词修饰）

    Magna cura id scripsit. He wrote it with great care.

  3. 时间

    Eo tempore id scripsit. He wrote it at that time.

    Una hora id scripsit. He wrote it in an hour.

  4. 分离

    Metu eos liberavit. He freed them from fear.

SUM (to be)

不定式 esse

直陈式现在时 单数 sum (I am); es (U are); est (s/he/it is; there is) 复数 sumus (we are); estis (U are); sunt (they are, there are)

直陈式将来时 单数 ero (I shall be); eris (you will be); erit (s/he/it will be) 复数 erimus (we will be); eritis (you will be ); erunt (they will be)

直陈式未完成时 单数 eram (I was ...); eras (you were ...); erat (s/he/it was) 复数 eramus (we will be); eratis (you will be ); erant (they will be)
